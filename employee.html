JOB FORM

Fill out the form below to apply for positions with American Labor Company. We will contact you soon to provide an update. The ALC hiring process is entirely free to someone who is seeking employment. Beneath the form is information on the next steps of the hiring process.

THE HIRING PROCESS

  1. Contact Us

    Apply for jobs using the contact form above.

  2. Interview

    Someone from the ALC team will reach out to set up an interview with you.

  3. Placement

    Following your interview, the ALC team will try to match you to opportunities based on your skillset, credentials, and needs. We will work hard to find a placement that ensures the best fit.

  4. First Day

    Once you are placed at a company, you will begin working full-time. That doesn’t mean your first day will be the last time communicating with ALC. There will be a trial period to ensure you and your employer are satisfied. Throughout the trial period, the ALC team will be in close contact with you.

EMPLOYEE FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • What industries can I find a job in?

    American Labor Company specializes in placing people in the skilled labor market. We have opportunities for HVAC professionals, electricians, machine operators, plumbers, welders, general laborers and many more!

  • How long does it take to find a job?

    It depends. Every trade and company are different and have their own process. Our team will follow up as soon as we receive news to provide you with updates.

  • What happens if I do not like the position?

    American Labor Company is committed to providing the best match possible. Our partnered companies offer at-will employment; this means that if you are not satisfied with your current position, you are free to walk away at any time. If you wish for another placement, we will work with you to find a better fit.
