FIND EMPLOYEE FORM

Fill out the form below to let us know how we can help you! We will contact you soon to provide an update. Beneath the form is information on our process and frequently asked questions.

TALENT SEARCH PROCESS

  1. Contact Us

    Let us know your skilled labor requirements through the form above. The information you provide gets sent to our team who will begin the process.

  2. Meeting

    The ALC team will reach out to set up a meeting. The purpose of this meeting is to better understand your needs as a company so we can provide you with the best employees for your business.

  3. Placement

    Following our meeting, the ALC team will offer you candidates based on your specifications. There will be a time period between our meeting and our talent recommendations to ensure the best fit.

  4. Trial Period

    After you make a hiring decision, there will be a trial period to ensure you are satisfied with your new employees. Furthermore, ALC be in touch with your new hire to ensure they are meeting your expectations and it is a good match.

EMPLOYER FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • Where do we source our talent from?

    We scour high and low to find you the best candidates. Our talentpool has a diverse background. Our candidates backgrounds include recent graduates from local vocational schools, candidates who have experience in the trades but are looking for a new employer, and motivated individuals entering the trades for their first time.

  • What industries do you specialize in?

    American Labor Company specializes in placing people in the skilled labor market. More specifically, we help companies find HVAC professionals, electricians, machine operators, plumbers, welders, general laborers and other positions.

  • How long does it take to find employees?

    There will be a time period between our meeting and placement. The reason for this wait is that we want to provide you with the best fit possible.

  • What happens if the employee is not working out?

    American Labor Company is committed to providing the best match possible. For this reason, we encourage our partnered companies to hire at-will. This means that if you are not satisfied with your employee, you can walk away anytime.

  • How much does your service cost?

    American Labor Company firmly believes that companies should not pay a dime unless they are satisfied with our service. If you onboard an ALC recommended employee and you decide to walk away within the first 30 days, you will not be charged a placement fee.
